The World's First National Park

Marvel at the many wonders Yellowstone National Park has to offer including geysers, waterfalls, boiling mud, wildlife, and more! The park itself encompasses 3, 472 square miles and is home to over 10,000 hydrothermal features that include geysers, hot springs, mud pots and fumaroles.

Or stop by and visit the largest high elevation lake in North America, Yellowstone Lake, which has 131.7 square miles of surface area and 141 miles of shoreline. Yellowstone is also home to the largest concentration of mammals in the lower 48 states (67) and is home to 285 different species of birds!
