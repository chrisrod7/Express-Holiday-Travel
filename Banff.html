Canada's First National Park​

Plan a trip to the oldest National park in Canada that is Banff National Park in "The Energy Province" of Alberta. The park itself is 2,564 square miles and offers hiking trails throughout the park that cover more than 1,000 miles, which are suitable for walking, biking, and horse riding.​

With over 33 different activities to plan and do within the park, there's something for everyone. From traditional park activities like hiking, camping, and kayaking, there's also activities such as Ice climbing, Scuba diving, and downhill skiing!
